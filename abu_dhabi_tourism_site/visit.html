What to do in Abu Dhabi?

Explore the stunning Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque, enjoy the thrilling rides at Ferrari World, and relax on the beautiful Corniche Beach. Don't miss the cultural experiences at the Louvre Abu Dhabi and Qasr Al Watan.

  • Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque
  • Ferrari World
  • Corniche Beach
  • Louvre Abu Dhabi
  • Qasr Al Watan

Getting to Abu Dhabi

Abu Dhabi is well-connected by air, with Abu Dhabi International Airport serving as the main gateway. You can also reach Abu Dhabi by road from Dubai and other parts of the UAE.

By Air

Abu Dhabi International Airport is the main airport serving the city, with flights from major international destinations.

By Road

Abu Dhabi is easily accessible by road from Dubai and other parts of the UAE. The journey from Dubai takes about 1.5 hours by car.
