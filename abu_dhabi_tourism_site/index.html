The Capital of the United Arab Emirates

Abu Dhabi

Abu Dhabi is the capital of the United Arab Emirates and the largest of the seven emirates. It is the center of government and business life in the UAE, headquarters of the emirates oil operating companies and embassies are based in the city. The city is characterized by its modern architecture, luxury shopping, and a vibrant nightlife scene. The city is also home to the famous Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque, one of the largest mosques in the world.

Climate

Abu Dhabi has a hot desert climate with high temperatures and sunny skies throughout the year. The city experiences very little rainfall, with most of it occurring during the winter months. The summer months are extremely hot, with temperatures often exceeding 40 degrees Celsius.

Things to Do

Abu Dhabi offers a wide range of activities and attractions for visitors to enjoy. Some of the top things to do in the city include:

  • Visit the Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque
  • Explore the Louvre Abu Dhabi
  • Visit the Emirates Palace
  • Go shopping at the Galleria Mall
  • Experience the thrill of Ferrari World
